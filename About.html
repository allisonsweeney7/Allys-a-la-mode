Our Story


Ally's à la Mode is a family company that started up back in 2014. It all began when I was a little girl spending the summer at my grandmother's house while my parents were working. We would spend our time together always creating fun new recipes in the kitchen. For months, we perfected our cherry pie recipe and always took the opportunity to share our creations with our family. In May of 2013, my grandma and I began dreaming up a plan for a future storefront. After eight years we are loving every moment we've spent together in the shop.
